About

KSV Mahal AC in Madipakkam is an air-conditioned venue with a seating capacity of 225 and a floating capacity of 340. It offers generator backup, kitchen facilities, lift access, and air-conditioned rooms for guests. Outdoor catering is also allowed, making it a convenient choice for events like weddings and receptions.
